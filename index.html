Spectacular Stays

Curating the world's most stunning and unique hotel experiences

Spectacular Stays was created by Jonathan after he grew exasperated with travel blogs being dialuted with sponsored posts. He came up with the idea of creating a purpose built platform on wordpress allowing bloggers to write sponsored guest posts. The site has succeeded in attracting over 40 talented travel bloggers, photographers and video experts as well as engaging over 80 hotels. Together they have created a highly visual experience, enticing readers to find out more about each individual hotel.

Visit the site at spectacualrstays.com as well as our new sister site that we have recently launched in Chinese, baityu.com.

The Live Lockdown

2 hour live music show on K2K radio

The Live Lockdown is a radio show started by Jonathan that brought together some of the best musicians in London to produce a 2 hour live show on Kilburn to Kensal Radio in London.

The show also attracted listener Roger Trowbridge, musical archavist and president of the National Harmonica Association, who collaborated with Jonathan to produce a one-off musical documentary of British Blues Harmonica pioneer Cyril Davies, which can now be found on the official Cyril Davies website.

You can listen back to one of the shows below or find them all here. The show is currently on haitus as Jonathan juggles other priorities but is due to kick off again in early 2015.

Startup Institute London Student Blog

Reflections and case studies from students attending
the 8 week program at Startup Institute in Fall 2014

The Startup Institute is a career accelerator that combines hard skills with cultural acumen to drastically increase the impact of startup employees.

The student blog was set up by Jonathan to allow participants on the program to impart their reflections and lessons learnt during the course of the 8 weeks they spent learning, networking and navigating the startup scene in London.
